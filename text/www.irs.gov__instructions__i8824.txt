Instructions for Form 8824 (2025)
Source: https://www.irs.gov/instructions/i8824

- These are the IRS instructions for Form 8824, Like-Kind Exchanges.
- Use them for reporting exchanges of business or investment real property, determining realized and recognized gain, tracking boot and liabilities, and computing basis in replacement property.
- This instruction set is directly relevant to property-transaction benchmark questions involving section 1031 exchanges.
