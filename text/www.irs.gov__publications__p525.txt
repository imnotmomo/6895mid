Publication 525 (2025), Taxable and Nontaxable Income
Source: https://www.irs.gov/publications/p525

- Publication 525 is the IRS guide to taxable and nontaxable income.
- Use it for questions about whether particular receipts are included in gross income, excluded from income, or reported in a special way.
- This publication is often relevant for fringe benefits, canceled debts, settlements, pensions, unemployment compensation, and other miscellaneous income items.
