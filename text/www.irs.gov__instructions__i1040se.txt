Instructions for Schedule E (Form 1040) (2025)
Source: https://www.irs.gov/instructions/i1040se

- These are the IRS instructions for Schedule E (Form 1040), Supplemental Income and Loss.
- Use them for rental real estate, royalties, pass-through income from partnerships and S corporations, estates and trusts, REMIC items, and related reporting rules.
- This instruction set is especially relevant for rental-property and passive-activity questions in individual returns.
