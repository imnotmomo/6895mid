Tax information, tools, and resources for businesses and self- employed. Run your business Business types Sole proprietorship Someone who owns an unincorporated business by themselves. Partnership A relationship existing between two or more persons who join to carry on a trade or business. International business Foreign businesses with activities in the U.S. or domestic businesses with activities outside the U.S. Corporation A legal entity that is separate and distinct from its owners. S corporation Corporations that elect to pass corporate income, losses, deductions, and credits through to their shareholders for federal tax purposes. Limited Liability Company or LLC A corporate structure whereby the members of the company cannot be held personally liable for the company's debts or liabilities.
