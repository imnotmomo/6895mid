Publication 554 (2025), Tax Guide for Seniors
Source: https://www.irs.gov/publications/p554

- Publication 554 is the IRS tax guide for seniors.
- Use it for questions involving older taxpayers, retirement income, Social Security benefits, pensions, IRA distributions, credits, filing thresholds, and recordkeeping issues commonly faced by seniors.
- This publication is especially useful when the taxpayer is retired or receiving benefits from multiple retirement-related sources.
