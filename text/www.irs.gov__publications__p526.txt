Publication 526 (2025), Charitable Contributions
Source: https://www.irs.gov/publications/p526

- Publication 526 is the IRS guide to charitable contributions.
- Use it for questions about whether a donation is deductible, what substantiation is required, how contribution limits work, and how different kinds of property gifts are treated.
- This publication is useful for cash donations, noncash donations, appraisal/documentation issues, and carryover questions.
