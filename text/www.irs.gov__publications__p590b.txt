Publication 590-B (2025), Distributions from Individual Retirement Arrangements (IRAs)
Source: https://www.irs.gov/publications/p590b

- Publication 590-B is the IRS guide to distributions from individual retirement arrangements (IRAs).
- Use it for questions about taxable and nontaxable IRA distributions, early distribution rules, rollovers, conversions, basis recovery, and beneficiary/distribution issues.
- This publication is most useful when the question is about taking money out of an IRA rather than contributing to one.
