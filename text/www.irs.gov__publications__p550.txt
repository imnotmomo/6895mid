Publication 550 (2025), Investment Income and Expenses
Source: https://www.irs.gov/publications/p550

- Publication 550 is the IRS guide to investment income and expenses.
- Use it for questions about interest, dividends, capital gains and losses, basis reporting, mutual funds, wash sales, short sales, and investment expense treatment.
- This publication is especially useful for taxable brokerage activity and securities transactions.
