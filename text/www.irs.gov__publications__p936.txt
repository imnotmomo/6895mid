Publication 936 (2025), Home Mortgage Interest Deduction
Source: https://www.irs.gov/publications/p936

- Publication 936 is the IRS guide to the home mortgage interest deduction.
- Use it for questions about qualified residence interest, acquisition indebtedness, points, refinancings, home equity debt, and mortgage interest reporting.
- This publication is useful for owner-occupied homes, second homes, mortgage limitation rules, and Schedule A home-interest issues.
