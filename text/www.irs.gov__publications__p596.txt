Publication 596 (2025), Earned Income Credit (EIC)
Source: https://www.irs.gov/publications/p596

- Publication 596 is the IRS guide to the Earned Income Credit (EIC).
- Use it for questions about earned income, qualifying children, disqualifying investment income, filing status restrictions, and due diligence around the credit.
- This publication is relevant when the taxpayer may qualify for the EIC or when an answer depends on residency, age, or relationship tests.
