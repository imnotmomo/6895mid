Publication 501 (2025), Dependents, Standard Deduction, and Filing Information
Source: https://www.irs.gov/publications/p501

- Publication 501 is the IRS guide for dependents, the standard deduction, and filing information.
- Use it for questions about filing status, who can be claimed as a dependent, standard deduction rules, and age/blindness add-ons.
- This publication is often relevant when the user asks about whether someone qualifies as a dependent or whether a taxpayer should itemize or use the standard deduction.
