Electronic filing now available for Form 1040-X You can now file Form 1040-X electronically with tax filing software to amend your Form 1040, 1040-SR, or 1040-NR for the current or two prior tax periods. Paper filing is still an option for Form 1040-X. File Form 1040-X to: - Correct Form 1040, 1040-SR, or 1040-NR (or older filings of Form 1040-A or 1040-EZ). - Make certain elections after the deadline. - Change amounts previously adjusted by the IRS. - Make a claim for a carryback due to a loss or unused credit.
