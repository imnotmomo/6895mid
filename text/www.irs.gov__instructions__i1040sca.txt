Instructions for Schedule A (Form 1040) (2025)
Source: https://www.irs.gov/instructions/i1040sca

- These are the IRS instructions for Schedule A (Form 1040), Itemized Deductions.
- Use them for medical expense deductions, taxes paid, home mortgage interest, gifts to charity, casualty and theft losses where applicable, and other itemized deduction line-by-line rules.
- This instruction set is valuable when a question turns on how an amount is entered on Schedule A or what documentation/limitations apply to a line item.
