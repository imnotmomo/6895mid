Instructions for Form 8949 (2025)
Source: https://www.irs.gov/instructions/i8949

- These are the IRS instructions for Form 8949, Sales and Other Dispositions of Capital Assets.
- Use them for broker statement adjustments, basis reporting categories, digital asset transaction boxes, short-term versus long-term sales, and the transfer of totals to Schedule D.
- This instruction set is essential when the issue is detailed reporting of capital asset sales.
