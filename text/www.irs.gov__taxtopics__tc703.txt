Topic No. 703 Basis of Assets
Source: https://www.irs.gov/taxtopics/tc703

- Topic no. 703 explains basis of assets.
- It states that basis is generally the amount paid for the asset and that basis is used to compute depreciation, casualty losses, and gain or loss on sale or exchange.
- Use this topic page as a concise routing source before going deeper into Publication 551 and property-disposition guidance.
