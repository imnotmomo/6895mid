Publication 505 (2025), Tax Withholding and Estimated Tax
Source: https://www.irs.gov/publications/p505

- Publication 505 is the IRS guide for withholding and estimated tax.
- Use it for questions about Form W-4 adjustments, quarterly estimated tax payments, underpayment safe harbors, and coordinating withholding across multiple jobs or income sources.
- This publication is useful when the user asks how much to withhold, whether estimated payments are needed, or how to avoid underpayment penalties.
