Topic No. 701 Sale of Your Home
Source: https://www.irs.gov/taxtopics/tc701

- Topic no. 701 explains the sale of your home.
- It highlights the home-sale exclusion and notes that a taxpayer may be able to exclude up to $250,000 of gain, or up to $500,000 on a joint return, if the requirements are met.
- Use this topic page as a concise home-sale routing source before going deeper into Publication 523 and Schedule D instructions.
