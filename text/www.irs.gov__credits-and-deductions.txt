You can use credits and deductions to help lower your tax bill or increase your refund. - Credits can reduce the amount of tax due. - Deductions can reduce the amount of taxable income. Credits and deductions are available for individuals and businesses. The Inflation Reduction Act of 2022 provides new and extended credit and deductions for individuals and businesses, tax-exempt and government entities.
