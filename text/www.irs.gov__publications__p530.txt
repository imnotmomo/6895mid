Publication 530 (2025), Tax Information for Homeowners
Source: https://www.irs.gov/publications/p530

- Publication 530 is the IRS tax information guide for homeowners.
- Use it for questions about ownership-related tax items such as real estate taxes, basis adjustments, points, mortgage interest, casualty impacts, and selling or using a home.
- This publication is helpful when the issue is homeowner tax treatment rather than rental-property operations.
