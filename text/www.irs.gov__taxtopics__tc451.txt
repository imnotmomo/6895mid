Topic No. 451 Individual Retirement Arrangements (IRAs)
Source: https://www.irs.gov/taxtopics/tc451

- Topic no. 451 explains individual retirement arrangements (IRAs).
- It states that an IRA is a tax-favored personal savings arrangement for retirement and notes that there are different types of IRAs, including traditional IRAs and Roth IRAs.
- Use this topic page as a concise routing source before going deeper into Publication 590-A or 590-B.
