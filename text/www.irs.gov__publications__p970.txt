Publication 970 (2025), Tax Benefits for Education
Source: https://www.irs.gov/publications/p970

- Publication 970 is the IRS guide to education tax benefits.
- Use it for questions about the American opportunity credit, lifetime learning credit, qualified tuition programs such as 529 plans, Coverdell ESAs, student loan interest, and education-related exclusions.
- This publication is useful for comparing education benefits and coordinating benefits so the same expense is not used twice.
