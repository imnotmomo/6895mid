Publication 503 (2025), Child and Dependent Care Expenses
Source: https://www.irs.gov/publications/p503

- Publication 503 is the IRS guide to child and dependent care expenses.
- Use it for questions about the child and dependent care credit, qualifying persons, work-related care expenses, provider identification, and filing limitations.
- This publication is relevant when taxpayers pay for daycare, before- or after-school care, or care for a dependent adult so they can work or look for work.
