Instructions for Schedule C (Form 1040) (2025)
Source: https://www.irs.gov/instructions/i1040sc

- These are the IRS instructions for Schedule C (Form 1040), Profit or Loss From Business.
- Use them for sole proprietorship income and expense reporting, car and truck expenses, depreciation cross-references, home office connections, inventory, cost of goods sold, and line-by-line Schedule C filing rules.
- This instruction set is useful for self-employment and side-business questions.
