Publication 502 (2025), Medical and Dental Expenses
Source: https://www.irs.gov/publications/p502

- Publication 502 is the IRS guide to medical and dental expenses.
- Use it for Schedule A medical expense questions, including what costs count as medical expenses and what costs are nondeductible personal expenses.
- This publication is useful for questions involving doctors, hospital care, prescriptions, insurance premiums in certain cases, transportation for medical care, and long-term care items.
