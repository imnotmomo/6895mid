Publication 590-A (2025), Contributions to Individual Retirement Arrangements (IRAs)
Source: https://www.irs.gov/publications/p590a

- Publication 590-A is the IRS guide to contributions to individual retirement arrangements (IRAs).
- Use it for questions about contribution eligibility, compensation requirements, deduction limits, Roth versus traditional IRA contribution rules, excess contributions, and rollovers into IRAs.
- This publication is especially important for contribution-year planning and deduction analysis.
