IRS newsroom article on reporting digital asset income.

The IRS says any income earned from digital asset transactions must be reported on the federal tax return. Examples mentioned by the IRS include cryptocurrency, stablecoins, non-fungible tokens, and rewards income from staking or earn programs.

The IRS states that taxpayers filing Form 1040, 1040-SR, 1040-NR, 1041, 1065, 1120, or 1120-S must answer the digital asset question and report earned income from digital asset transactions.
