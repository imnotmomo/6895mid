Publication 969 (2025), Health Savings Accounts and Other Tax-Favored Health Plans
Source: https://www.irs.gov/publications/p969

- Publication 969 is the IRS guide to health savings accounts and other tax-favored health plans.
- Use it for questions about HSAs, Archer MSAs, Medicare Advantage MSAs, health FSAs, and HRAs, including contribution limits, distributions, rollovers, and excess-contribution issues.
- This publication is relevant when a taxpayer asks about high-deductible health plan eligibility, HSA deductions, or whether a distribution is taxable.
