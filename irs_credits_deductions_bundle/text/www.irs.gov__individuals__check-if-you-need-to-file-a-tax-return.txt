In this series: How to file your taxes: step by step Check if you need to file Gather your documents Get credits and deductions File your return Get your refund Pay taxes on time Be ready to file taxes next year Who must file Most U.S. citizens or permanent residents who work in the U.S. have to file a tax return. Generally, you need to file if: - Your income is over the filing requirement - You have over $400 in net earnings from self-employment (side jobs or other independent work) - You had other situations that require you to file It might pay you to file even if you don’t have to.
