Use Form 4506-T to request any of the transcripts: tax return, tax account, wage and income, record of account and verification of non-filling. The transcript format better protects taxpayer data by partially masking personally identifiable information. Financial data remains fully visible to allow for tax preparation, tax representation, or income verification. Because the taxpayer identification number is masked, there is a field on the Form 4506-T PDF to enter a customer file number which will display on the transcript.
